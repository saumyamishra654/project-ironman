Rules
  • Separate dairy from iron mealscalcium blocks iron absorption
  • Lemon on iron-rich foodvitamin C boosts absorption 2–6x
  • No tea/coffee with mealstannins block iron
  • More rice on heavy dayslong run, bench day — add 1 extra cup
  • Coconut water daily (200 ml)potassium + hydration — extra on long run days
  • Amla candy after dinnervitamin C + iron absorption from dinner eggs/rice
  • Magnesium at nighthelps sleep quality
  • Cod liver oil with fat-containing mealfat-soluble vitamins need dietary fat
  • 6 eggs per day is fineno cardiovascular concern for active individuals
