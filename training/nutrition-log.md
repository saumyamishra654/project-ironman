# Nutrition Log

Log meals here daily from June 15 onwards. Keep it brief — just what you ate, roughly when, and any notes.

---

## How to Use

1. **Daily:** add an entry with what you ate at each meal
2. **Weekly (Sunday night):** note bodyweight, energy levels, and any changes
3. **Flag anything unusual:** missed meals, gut issues on runs, low energy, cravings
4. **The AI reads this file** — mention patterns ("felt flat on Wednesday run", "skipped lunch twice this week") and it can adjust recommendations

### Template

```
### [Date] — [Day]
- **Breakfast:** 
- **Lunch:** 
- **Snack:** 
- **Dinner:** 
- **Supplements:** all / missed ___
- **Water:** roughly ___ L
- **Coconut water:** yes / no
- **Amla:** yes / no
- **Notes:** 
```
